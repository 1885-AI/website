## Products (equal billing)

### 1. Business Intelligence Layer
- Description: BI layer that sits on top of the dealership DMS.
- Capabilities: Dashboards, reporting, fast record access, AI chatbot over dealership data.
- Primary DMS: Lightspeed (CDK Lightspeed EVO / NXT).

### 2. CRM
- Description: Full dealership CRM. Standalone product; best paired with the BI layer.
- OEM support: Accepts ADF/XML lead feeds from authorized OEM lead providers. (Supported today / in development for pilot.)
- Primary DMS integration: Lightspeed.
- Seeking authorized-vendor approval from: all major powersports OEMs.

## Audience
- Primary buyer: Dealer Principal / Owner
- Primary users: Sales Manager, General Manager, Salespeople
- Secondary audience (for OEM vendor review): OEM vendor-approval teams

## Pain points by role
- Salesperson: Hard to keep lead status current. Poor reporting. Weak task management. Needs next-best-action guidance. Juggles 3–4 tools (personal cell, CRM app, etc.) to reach the customer.
- Sales Manager / GM: Poor visibility into what salespeople are working on and which hot prospects need follow-up. Can't tell which lead sources actually convert.
- Owner / Dealer Principal: Must stay compliant with every OEM's CRM requirements. Struggles to get usable data out of the DMS.
